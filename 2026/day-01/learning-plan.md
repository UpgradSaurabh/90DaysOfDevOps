# 90 Days DevOps Learning Plan

##Updated Day 01 learning plan.

## Current Level

I am currently working as a DevOps/CI-CD support engineer and have experience with GitLab pipelines, SonarQube integration, troubleshooting build failures, and deployment-related tasks.

---

## What is Your Understanding of DevOps and Cloud Engineering?

According to me, DevOps is the practice of improving collaboration between development and operations teams through automation, continuous integration, continuous delivery, monitoring, and faster deployments.

Cloud engineering helps organizations build scalable, reliable, and automated infrastructure using cloud platforms and modern tools.

---

## Why Are You Starting Learning DevOps & Cloud?

I want to become a complete DevOps engineer capable of independently handling CI/CD, Kubernetes, cloud infrastructure, automation, and production deployments.

My main focus is also to explore AI Agentic workflows and understand how AI agents can be integrated into day-to-day DevOps activities to improve productivity and automation.

Additionally, I want to grow professionally and achieve better career opportunities and salary growth.

---

## Where Do You Want to Reach?

By the end of this 90-day challenge, I want to become confident in DevOps interviews and gain hands-on experience with modern DevOps and cloud tools.

I also want to build practical projects and real-world use cases that I can confidently explain during interviews and apply in my daily work.

---

## Core Skills I Want to Build

- Kubernetes and container orchestration
- CI/CD pipeline automation and troubleshooting
- Cloud infrastructure and AI-driven DevOps workflows

---

## My 90-Day Goals

1. Build and deploy applications using Docker and Kubernetes
2. Learn cloud and infrastructure automation fundamentals
3. Explore AI Agentic workflows for DevOps automation

---

## Weekly Time Commitment

- Weekdays: 2 hours daily
- Weekends: 4–5 hours for practice and projects
- Additional learning time based on project requirements and availability

---

## Consistency Plan

I will maintain daily learning discipline by completing at least one DevOps topic or hands-on task every day and by tracking my progress publicly throughout the challenge.
